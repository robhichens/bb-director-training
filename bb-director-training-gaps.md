# BB Director Training — Competency Tracker Gap Analysis

**Generated:** May 2026  
**Scope:** 26 categories · 349 competency items vs. 7 training modules  
**Purpose:** Identify competencies in the tracker that receive little or no dedicated training, so future module updates or supplemental resources can close the gaps.

---

## Executive Summary

The training program covers the most visible director workflows well — daily operations, hiring, family communication, compliance, and billing fundamentals. However, **six categories totaling 102 items have no meaningful module coverage**, and an additional **nine categories have significant procedural gaps** where topics are named but not trained to a working level. The largest unaddressed areas are:

- **Payroll mechanics** (15 items) — how to run a pay period, corrections, and rounding rules
- **Desktop & platform setup** (38 items) — tool-by-tool access and configuration
- **Matrixes** (6 items) — location files mentioned nowhere in training
- **Assistant Director** (8 items) — no coverage of AD role, duties, or development
- **Infant care procedures** (13 items) — safe sleep, diapering, bottle handling, infant checklists
- **ProCare staff module** (14 items) — uploading employees, Brivo access, entering hours

## Category-by-Category Analysis

### 1. Foundation & Philosophy · 2 items
**Coverage: ✅ Strong** — Module 1  
Culture, core values, mission, and the A/B/C/D framework are the backbone of Module 1. Minimal gap risk.

---

### 2. Org Chart & Reporting Structure · 6 items
**Coverage: 🟡 Partial** — Module 1  
Module 1 establishes cultural hierarchy and general expectations. However, the specific reporting chain (Area Manager, ownership structure, who approves what), org chart documents, and escalation paths for operational decisions are not explicitly trained.

**Gaps:**
- Who to call for specific categories of problem (maintenance vs. HR vs. Area Manager)
- Ownership/franchise accountability structure
- Written org chart familiarization exercise

---

### 3. Meetings · 2 items
**Coverage: ✅ Strong** — Module 3  
Staff meeting cadence and format are covered. No significant gap.

---

### 4. Calendars · 5 items
**Coverage: 🟡 Partial** — Module 4  
Monthly planning calendar concept is covered in Module 4. However, the mechanics of *managing* the calendar system (which tools to use, how to publish to staff, how ProCare and Google Calendar interact) are not trained.

**Gaps:**
- Google Calendar setup and staff access
- ProCare calendar vs. classroom app calendar distinction
- Publishing and communicating the monthly calendar to families

---

### 5. Desktop & Platform Access · 38 items ⚠️
**Coverage: 🔴 Minimal** — Module 7 (reference only)  
Module 7 provides a links/access reference sheet, but no module teaches how to *set up, configure, or troubleshoot* any platform. With 38 items, this is the largest single category in the tracker and the one where a new director is most likely to feel lost on day one.

**Specific gaps (examples):**
- Brivo security system setup and access management
- FieldPrint location code and pre-employment screening workflow
- Canva brand template access and usage
- Google Workspace folder structure (Shared Drive organization)
- GroupMe setup and staff group configuration
- STARS/ChildPlus portal navigation
- iNtellikids / LineLeader daily sheet configuration
- Tadpoles classroom setup and family app onboarding
- Vector alarm system codes and procedures
- Camera/monitor system operation
- Network/Wi-Fi admin access

**Recommendation:** A dedicated "Day-1 Platform Orientation" section or Module 7 expansion covering each system's login, purpose, and first-use steps.

---

### 6. Daily Routine · 37 items
**Coverage: 🟡 Partial** — Module 2  
Module 2 covers the director's daily arc well (morning message, playground check, AM duties, closing) and the weekly rhythm. However, 37 items is expansive and several procedural specifics fall through.

**Gaps:**
- VBQ5 / CLASS observation scheduling and documentation workflow
- DOR (Director Operations Report) as a *managed system* — not just mentioned
- End-of-day closing procedures step by step (not just the checklist concept)
- Substitute coverage procedures when ratio is at risk
- Weather/indoor protocol decision-making

---

### 7. Reporting & Documentation · 8 items
**Coverage: 🟡 Partial** — Modules 2, 4, 5  
Several report types are mentioned across modules. But the full reporting suite is not trained together or as a workflow.

**Gaps:**
- DOR completion and submission cadence
- VBQ5 walkthrough and scoring interpretation
- Licensing visit documentation protocol
- Injury/incident report chain of custody (who gets a copy, how long to retain)

---

### 8. Planning & Curriculum Records · 12 items
**Coverage: 🔴 Minimal** — Module 5  
Module 5 briefly references licensing requirements for curriculum files. No module trains the director on how to manage the curriculum planning process.

**Gaps:**
- Lesson plan submission and review process
- Curriculum scope-and-sequence documentation
- CACFP menu documentation requirements
- Classroom observation notes as a management tool
- Portfolio / developmental record expectations

---

### 9. Tours & Enrollment · 25 items
**Coverage: ✅ Strong** — Module 2  
Tour flow, enrollment process, and waitlist management are covered in Module 2 with scripted talking points. This is one of the best-trained areas.

**Minor gaps:**
- ACH-only payment rule (no cash/check) — stated in competency tracker but not emphasized in training
- Deposit handling procedures (receipt, recording, refund policy)
- Enrollment packet review checklist (which forms are mandatory before first day)

### 10. Phone & Customer Service · 12 items
**Coverage: ✅ Strong** — Module 2  
Phone script, professional tone expectations, and inquiry handling are covered. Minimal gap.

**Minor gap:**
- Handling difficult or escalating calls (when to involve Area Manager) — covered in Module 6 scripts but not in the phone section

**Gaps:**
- Google Business Profile management (updating hours, responding to reviews, adding photos)
- Facebook/Instagram post cadence and content guidelines
- Canva template selection and brand-compliant design
- Indeed job posting management
- Monthly marketing calendar or posting schedule
- Email newsletter or parent communication campaigns

### 12. Professionalism & Conduct · 5 items
**Coverage: ✅ Strong** — Module 1  
Dress code, professional demeanor, and admin standards are covered. Minimal gap.

### 13. Hiring · 24 items

Module 3 covers the hiring process well at a conceptual level. Several procedural specifics are missing.

**Gaps:**
- FieldPrint pre-employment screening — exact steps and location code entry
- Indeed posting setup and sponsored vs. organic listings
- Interview question bank (legal vs. illegal questions)
- Offer letter template and conditional offer language
- VA background check registry (CPS Central Registry) submission steps
- Reference check documentation requirements

### 14. New Employee Onboarding · 13 items
**Coverage: 🟡 Partial** — Module 3  
New hire checklist concept is covered. ProCare staff module and system access provisioning are not.

**Gaps:**
- ProCare employee record creation (see also Category 21)
- Brivo access card assignment and PIN setup
- Tadpoles/classroom app teacher onboarding
- State-required orientation documentation timing
- 90-day check-in cadence for new hires

### 15. New Families · 8 items
**Coverage: 🟡 Partial** — Module 6  
Module 6 covers the family relationship philosophy and trust-building. First-week procedural specifics are not trained.

**Gaps:**
- Welcome call within 7 days of enrollment (not just mentioned — scripted and practiced)
- First 90-day milestone gifts/check-in schedule
- ACH setup confirmation before first day
- New family packet walk-through script
- Communication preference collection (app vs. email vs. phone)

---

### 16. Staff Day-to-Day Management · 22 items
**Coverage: 🟡 Partial** — Modules 1, 3  
Module 1 covers minimums and Module 3 covers performance management. Specific daily enforcement scenarios are thin.

**Gaps:**
- Cell phone policy enforcement (in bin, off) — mentioned in Module 1 minimums but no enforcement guidance
- Call-out protocol and call-in chain of command
- Staff meal break documentation
- SOL (Statement of Limitation) procedure and when it applies
- Sprinkler/water play documentation and approval steps

---

### 17. Assistant Director · 8 items ⛔
**Coverage: ⛔ None**  
The Assistant Director role is not addressed in any of the 7 training modules. This is a significant gap given that the AD is typically the director's primary operational backup and is critical to continuity of care.

**Specific unaddressed items:**
- AD duties and authority scope
- AD development and goal-setting cadence
- Delegating tasks to the AD appropriately
- AD as primary substitute when director is out
- AD performance review structure
- AD promotion pathway expectations

**Recommendation:** Add a dedicated AD section to Module 3 (People Management) or create a standalone reference.

---

### 18. Infant Care · 13 items ⚠️
**Coverage: 🔴 Minimal** — Module 5  
Module 5 covers general health and safety, with some reference to SIDS/safe sleep in the classroom setup section. There is no dedicated infant care training.

**Specific unaddressed items:**
- Diapering procedure steps and documentation
- Bottle preparation and storage protocol
- Safe sleep policy enforcement (including parent waiver process)
- Infant feeding documentation (breastmilk storage, formula tracking)
- Infant-specific opening/closing checklist
- Developmental milestone tracking for infants
- Parent communication expectations for infant room

**Recommendation:** Add an Infant Room Operations section to Module 5 or a standalone infant care reference document.

---

### 19. Children's Files · 5 items
**Coverage: 🟡 Partial** — Module 5  
Licensing binder requirements are covered. Children's file internal organization less so.

**Gaps:**
- Required documents for active file vs. archived file
- Photo/video consent form tracking
- IEP/IFSP documentation handling
- STARS enrollment record requirements

---

### 20. ProCare: Billing · 19 items
**Coverage: 🟡 Partial** — Module 4  
Module 4 covers billing mechanics, tuition, and collections at a working level. Specific ledger operations are less thorough.

**Gaps:**
- Ledger adjustment and credit/refund procedures
- Subsidy billing (CCAP) workflow and documentation
- Late fee assessment and waiver process
- iNtellikids / LineLeader daily integration with ProCare
- Month-end billing close process
- Past-due account escalation steps (first notice → final notice → termination)

---

### 21. ProCare: Staff Module · 14 items ⚠️
**Coverage: ⛔ None** (as a distinct workflow)  
Module 3 mentions ProCare for time-tracking context, but the Staff module in ProCare — creating employee records, uploading documents, managing timecards — is not trained anywhere.

**Specific unaddressed items:**
- Creating and activating a new employee record
- Uploading required documents to employee file in ProCare
- Configuring clock-in/out permissions
- Running timesheets and resolving missed punches
- Entering hours for salaried vs. hourly staff
- Exporting timesheet data for payroll processor

**Recommendation:** Add a ProCare Staff Module walkthrough to Module 3 or Module 7.

---

### 22. Financial Reports · 9 items
**Coverage: 🟡 Partial** — Module 4  
KPIs and billing are covered. Reading financial reports as a director is not.

**Gaps:**
- P&L (Profit & Loss) statement interpretation
- Labor cost percentage calculation and target benchmarks
- Supply budget tracking and monthly reconciliation
- Revenue vs. enrollment capacity analysis
- Comparing actuals to budget in Area Manager review

---

### 23. Compliance & Licensing · 15 items
**Coverage: ✅ Strong** — Module 5  
Virginia DSS licensing, binder maintenance, health/safety protocols, and emergency procedures are the core of Module 5. This is one of the strongest areas.

**Minor gaps:**
- Unannounced licensing visit protocol (director behavior and staff communication during visit)
- STARS renewal process and documentation timeline

---

### 24. Payroll · 15 items ⛔
**Coverage: ⛔ None**  
Module 3 covers scheduling and staff ratios. Payroll as a system — how a pay period works, who processes it, what the director's role is — is not trained at all.

**Specific unaddressed items:**
- Pay period cycle (dates, submission deadline)
- Rounding rules for time punches
- How to handle missed punches before cutoff
- Live check vs. direct deposit procedures
- Payroll correction/reversal process
- OT approval and documentation
- Submitting hours to the payroll processor
- Holiday pay and PTO payout handling

**Recommendation:** Add a Payroll Basics section to Module 4 (Business Operations).

---

### 25. Matrixes · 6 items ⛔
**Coverage: ⛔ None**  
Matrixes (location-specific reference files — tuition matrices, ratio matrices, subsidy rate matrices) are not mentioned in any training module.

**Specific unaddressed items:**
- Where to locate the current tuition matrix
- How the matrix is structured (age band × schedule type)
- Subsidy rate matrix and how it differs from private pay
- When to reference the matrix during tours vs. enrollment
- Who updates the matrix and the approval process

**Recommendation:** Add a Matrixes orientation to Module 4 or Module 7.

---

### 26. Brand Standards & Facilities · 22 items
**Coverage: 🟡 Partial** — Modules 1, 6  
Module 1 covers brand culture and Module 6 covers marketing voice. Physical brand standards and facility operational specifics are not trained.

**Gaps:**
- Bulletin board standards (brown paper, specific border colors/styles)
- Lobby and exterior appearance checklist
- Supply order deadline (23rd of month rule)
- Camera and monitor system operation
- Vector security system — arming/disarming procedures
- SOL (Statement of Limitation) facility postings
- Parking lot traffic flow and drop-off safety protocol
- Seasonal decoration standards and approval
